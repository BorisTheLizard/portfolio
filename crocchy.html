Crocchy

Classic retro wave style rocket adventure game

SMALL DESCRIPTION HERE Like Alice, you will venture down the rabbit hole leading into a world that one dares not to speak of aloud to your friends or family for the secrets of its chaos dare to be too much!

FAT DESCRIPTION HERE The Dictator gives you the unique chance to throw all your inhibitions away. Dare to push the boundaries of madness. After all, how many atrocities can one dictator commit? As we plunge head first into insanity, from the very first mission of blowing up buildings and homes of the innocent, you will progress through the game by convincing the masses with propaganda and leading to the fall of the people's freedom. Stand on the throats of the weak with drunken power to become the supreme ruler of the hell which only a dictator can fathom.

This project was created to demonstrate a different world, an excitingly terrible world full of violence, madness, and dark humor, which you have never seen or understood before.
