Crocchy

Classic retro style rocket adventure game

Get Crocchy NOW!

Crocchy is a classic rocket adventure game. You must pass the level without collisions to be able to complete it and go to the next scene. Don't forget to use your stasis module to make your way out easier ;)

Crocchy is a little alien who is rushing to go home and see his loved one and prepare for his wedding ceremony tomorrow. Unfortunately for our little friend, a giant portal appeared in front of his space ship and took him to the heart of an asteroid field and left him to survive on a little rescue rocket. To save Crocchys life and be able to return him to the loved one you need to be very careful because even a small collision of this rocket will provoke a big explosion! Big adventure awaits for Crocchy on his way home, many different biomes, creatures and difficulties.
